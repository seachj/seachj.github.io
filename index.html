Portfolio

NCAA Media Assets Page

Butler University hosted the 2021 NCAA Tournament at Hinkle Fieldhouse, which presented an opportunity to have the University featured in the media. To facilitate and encourage media coverage, I created a page which provided reporters with image and video assets, which they could easily view and download for use in their work.

Future Bulldog Campaign Landing Page

Using the Unbounce page builder, I created the landing page for the 2019 and 2020 Future Bulldog email campaigns. This campaign sent emails which drove prospective students to this landing page, which then prompted students to share their information with the University.

Butler Portal

While Butler's old website was active, one common complaint was that users had trouble navigating to certain tools and resources. To remedy this, I created a portal page to help users find what they need. This concept was refined and brought over to the current website as an item available at all times from the global navigation.
