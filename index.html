ALA Think Tank Report

For an ethnography project in one of my past classes, I researched a group called the ALA Think Tank, an unofficial social media space for librarians. I studied the group's web presences and how members use social media while examining the rhetorical aspects of the group's web presence. Then, after doing research and finding that libraries may struggle to engage communities and patrons, I compared ways the group uses social media to ways actual libraries might be able to use social media to connect with people in their local communities.

Research Paper:
"Erin Belieu's 'In the Red Dress I Wear to Your Funeral': Conveying Emotion by Manipulating Form"

In this paper, which I presented at the 2017 Clement S. Stacy Undergraduate Research Conference, I analyzed the language patterns in Erin Belieu's poem "In the Red Dress I Wear to Your Funeral". I noted that the increasingly chaotic and disintegrating language gave the poem a sense of growing intense emotion and found precedent for the use of chaotic language to convey emotion by researching modernist poems such as T.S. Eliot's "The Waste Land".
