Hoppuccino Rabbit Cafe

At Indiana's first rabbit cafe, you can help local rescue animals. A portion of all proceeds goes to local shelters, and at the cafe you can enjoy our food and drinks while spending time with the rabbits even if you don't have time for one at home. All rabbits are also available for adoption, should you find yourself getting attached.

All rabbits in our cafe are adoptable rescue rabbits waiting for a home.
